What are TimeMints?

Each of the 3,333 minting addresses known as TimeMints assigned during the contribution period mint additional DAY tokens based on their ChronoPower, a unique daily minting power rate.

TimeMint real estate is scarce and the ChronoPower of each successive TimeMint decreases from a max of 1% to 0.5% rewarding early contributors in a ChronoLogic fashion.

Time is immutable. Irreversible. It is value. However, ultimately its total supply is unknown.

Why Time?

ChronoLogic expands the possibilities of using time in blockchain projects to implement its features in fields such as finance, transportation & eCommerce.

ChronoLogic considers time as value, however, Ethereum was not originally designed for these exact use-cases resulting in several innovative concepts discussed in the whitepaper. In its first phase ChronoLogic pegs time to DAY -- a store of value token based on the Ethereum blockchain.
